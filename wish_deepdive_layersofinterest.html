Deep-Dives - Layers of Interest

Layers of Interest - Guide the Eye

Substitute for Depth Of Field (DoF) in photo-realistic approaches.

Depth/distance stays defined, but edges are lost and found, and colors and forms fade.

  • Object in focus:
    • Well defined
    • Lines
    • detailed
  • Out of focus:
    • monochromatic
    • 'Munged' (reduced by data blending)
    • More color-wash approach
    • Mass shapes more
