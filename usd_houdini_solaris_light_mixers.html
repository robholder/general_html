Light Mixers & Snapshots

Light Mixer(s) are a way of controlling all the lights from a single node.

  • In the tutorial, the lights are chained down the network spine.
  • The Light Mixer node is also in the network chain.
  • TAB > Light Mixer

In the Light Mixer parameters panel:

  • from the Light List (left):
    • select and drag lights into the right pane.

Red dots on a value field indicates a change in the Light Mixer.

Snapshots

Snapshots are a method of recording a state of the entire network in the stage.

To see the Snapshot panel:

  • In the Houdini Solaris Layout:
    • above the 'Scene Graph Tree' you can open a closed panel (using the panel expansion arrow).
    • This is the Snapshot panel

Snapshots record the state of all nodes in the Stage network (including parameter states and positions - including the camera).

To save a snapshot:

  • Adjust scene (including the Light Mixer node)
  • Click on the Snapshot icon (red push button icon)
  • Rename is necessary.

Double clicking on a thumbnail will open a preview window with render and details.

To assign or revert to a snapshot:

  • RMB on a snapshot and:
    • Revert Network to this Snapshot
    • All network nodes will revert to the state at snapshot
    • This will also work well with the state of the Light Mixer (to save states)
    • Caution with things adjustments like camera and other settings. They get saved/reverted too.

Delete snapshots:

  • LMB to select
  • <delete> key
