Deep-Dives - Stylization TechCheck List

https://gogo.disneyanimation.com/display/light/WISH%3A+Stylization+TechCheck+List

Key / Rim / Volume Tags

  • Check the light tagging:
    • Key:
      • Broad key lighting
      • Primary Munging areas
    • Rim:
      • These are lights that are slim hair-lights
      • Rims will get less Munging
      • Tight edge lights
    • Volume:
      • Adjust to the scene's volumes
      • There's an additional control to obliterate lines from volumes

Layers Of Interest

Are the layers properly defined in the shot (from the key-shot).

  • Define areas for detail diminishing:
    • Geo details
    • Lines
  • Can work the opposite way:
    • Bring back definition (from propagated shot perhaps)
    • Bring back or boost lines
  • Check Layers of Interest has color channels:
    • Look that they have R, G & B (not just alpha)
    • Fix with shuffle

Watercolor Paper

  • SetExt Paper:
    • Check it covers all the frame
    • (If broken - holes - contact SetExt)
    • Overlays should be:
      • 0.4
      • 0.7
    • Look for texture-swimming:
      • pRef might need toggling on to cure

Eye Specs

  • Check eyes have specs

Sharpen

  • If areas are getting mushy:
    • Sharpen if wide-shot
    • Disable broken edges if too much breakup

Noise & Aliasing

  • Check if Lines or Edge-pooling:
    • Lines:
      • Look through each 'line type' to look for issues
    • Edge-pooling:
      • Environment median can cause noise (remove if necessary)
  • Kuwuhara
  • Bilateral
  • BrokenEdgeSimple:
    • Enable Erode:
    • This option can be more noisy:
      • Check for line noise here

Edge Glowing (esp Hair)

  • Surface Color is used to color lines:
    • Lines can expand beyond geo
    • Dilate edges of character to compensate and match edge width

Debug any issues

  • Backtrack:
    • look for tags
    • Check visually the tagged layers (key & rim):
      • Are parts of the scene missing?
      • Are the render layers all in place?
  • Merges:
    • Must be A over B (layers come through B)
    • 'also merge': should be 'all'
