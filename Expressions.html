Expressions

  • Tip: Open a Textport panel:
    • >exhelp
    • This will give you help on all expressions!
  • exhelp padzero

The exhelp above will break down the padzero expression.

$HIP/render/shot_`padzero(4, 23)`.$F4.exr

  • exhelp opdigits

This exhellp above will break down the opdigits expression.

So, on a karmarenderproperties node with a trailing digit of '1' in the node name:

$HIP/render/shot_`padzero(4, opdigits("."))`.$F4.exr

...will expand to shot_001.0001.exr
