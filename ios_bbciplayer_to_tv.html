iOS - BBC iPlayer & ITVx app to TV

Physical Connections

  • USB-C to HDMI Adapter:
    • I have one integrated into the multiport (USB-A/Ethernet/HDMI)
  • HDMI from Adapter to TV
  • Select HDMI on TV interface.

iOS Settings for Physical TV Connection

  • Connect Bluetooth mouse
    • (or use USB-A adapter into USB-d multi-adapter)
  • After physical connection (iPad to TV):
  • iPad > Settings >Display & Brightness:
    • Extend Displays (not screen mirroring)
    • Arrange as needed
    • TV should be showing screen extended on HDMI TV channel/input

iPlayer

Physical Connection Needed

  • The iPlayer app needs a physical connection to show on TV (see above)
  • Use VPN app to set to UK (commercial account needed)
  • Open and Sign into iPlayer
  • Drag from bottom-right corner of iPlayer inwards slightly (to show as floating window)
    • Use mouse to drag iPlayer window to TV
    • Hover cursor/mouse over top-left corner of iPlayer to reveal the three window-size buttons
    • Click green to expand to fill TV
  • Dim iPad to conserve battery
  • Optionally plug USB-C power into multi-adapter
  • Control the app with the mouse

ITVx App

Physical Connection Optional

  • The ITVx app doesn't need a physical connection:
    • (although you can use one (as described above)
  • Use VPN app to set to UK (commercial account needed)
  • Open and Sign into the ITVx app
  • Start playing
  • Click on the AirPlay icon (usually top-right of video window):
    • Choose the TV as a target:
      • You might need to match the pin shown on TV
    • The app should show on TV with the correct resolution and sound
