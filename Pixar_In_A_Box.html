Lighting

  • Quality of Light:
    • Color
    • Intensity
    • Softness (size)
  • Small Light:
    • Harsh
    • Falloff
  • Large Light:
    • Soft
    • Less falloff

Light Setup

  • Key Light:
    • Shaping Light:
      • Gives dimension and shape
  • Fill Light:
    • Adds light into shadows
    • Example - sky
  • Bounce Light:
    • Bounces off surfaces into scene
    • Add bounce cards/objects
  • Rim Light:
    • Carves things out with an edge
  • Kick Light:
    • Separates subject from Background
    • Usually further forward than Rim
    • Sometimes motivated by something in the scene (TV, lamp, etc.)
