Nuke - Search and Replace (from Interface)

Search & Replace Hotkey

The hotkey to do a "search and replace" for any nodes selected: 

Ctrl + Shift + /

This is useful for changing rogue characters (like _% to .%, or alternate padding or paths).

Just select the nodes you want to evaluate, then <cntrl> + <shift> + /.
