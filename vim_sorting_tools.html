Sort

Vim has an inbuilt tool to sort:

  • :sort

Sort Whole Buffer

To sort the whole buffer: 

:sort.

To sort a range: 

:5,17 sort.

Options and Flags

Remove duplicate lines: 

dog
cat
mouse
pangolin
mouse

:sort u

Run :sort u to sort them and to remove the duplicates. Vim :sort does not sort numbers correctly. If you have: 2 donuts 1 donut 11 donut 10 donuts Running :sort will give you: 1 donut 10 donuts 11 donut 2 donuts
