AL_Proxy

  • AL_Proxy:
    • Animal Logic system is used to translate USD data to Maya
    • Everything from the USD data is under:
      • AL_Proxy
      • Keeps track of what's in Maya and USD stage
    • Do Not change anything under AL_Proxy:
      • Special case - light rigs
