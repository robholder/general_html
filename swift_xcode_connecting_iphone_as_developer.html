Swift - Connecting iPhone to Xcode

  • In Xcode, go to the top menu bar and select Window > Devices and Simulators (or press Cmd + Shift + 2).
  • Plug in the iPhone using a lightning cable to the MacOS device.
  • Select your iPhone in the left sidebar.
  • Look at the main panel. You will likely see a yellow bar or a message saying "Developer Mode is disabled" with a button or text saying "Enable in Settings."
  • Simply keeping this window open while your phone is plugged in sends a "ping" to the OS.
  • Go to the iPhone settings, and go almost to the bottom of the Privacy & Security page.
  • Switch Developer mode to 'on':
    • Accept the dialogs.
    • Restart
  • "Copying shared cache symbols from iPhone (% complete)" appears.
  • Keep phone awake.

Wireless

  • In Xcode:
    • Product > Scheme > Edit Scheme...
  • Turn off Debugger check-box to allow Wifi run from Xcode
