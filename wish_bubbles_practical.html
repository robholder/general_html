To Actually set up Layers and Mods

Creating the Bubble Setup

  • First, where there is a Render tab and a Tools tab (in DLight):
    • Reveal the Lighting tab by switching it on:
      • In the 'hamburger menu' (three short lines) between the regular DLight menu and the tabs (Render & Tools).
  • Navigate to (and select) the "lr_practical" lighting palette in the Lights panel.
  • On the right-most end of the Lighting tab (now to the right of the DLight menus), click on the artist's palette icon (with the blue dot):
    • This will populate:
      • Utility Layer
      • Insert Mods
  • Only separate emission categories on lights you need! (40-46, yes; others not too much):
    • skydome perhaps on (to get the spec etc.)
    • char lights perhaps on (to get the spec etc.)

  • Disconnect unwanted emissions (all the lights before the inner backdrops):
