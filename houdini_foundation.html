Navigating Node Graphs

  • Jump up:
    • Obj type path nav:
      • RMB to menu in here
    • U key
  • Jump down:
    • I key
    • RMB menu
  • F8:
    • Toggle Obj/Geometry
  • Tree View:
    • Click on items like Maya's outliner
    • Filter is at the base of the panel

Set Navigation Waypoints

  • Cntrl 2:
    • set waypoint at 2
  • 1:
    • Takes you back to the root
  • 2:
    • takes you to your custom waypoint #2

Align Nodes

  • Select nodes:
    • A and LMB Drag:
      • align vertical
      • align horizontal
  • Shift and LMB Drag:
    • Move nodes above (in chain)
  • Cntl and drag:
    • Move nodes below (in chain)

Dots & Wires

  • Dots:
    • Alt LMB
    • Draging them they become sticky and join other lines
    • Alt LMB while draging out a line will drop Dots on the fly
  • Shift S:
    • Toggles - Curved lines to angular style
  • Wires are selectable:
    • to delete
  • Re-wire by hovering over dots, and draging handle to another dot/node
  • Alt click on a dot to pin to position
  • Y LMB:
    • Slash away wired/connections
  • Color:
    • you can add color to dots by draging color onto dot
  • Long wire fading:
    • Mouse over to see at full strength
    • D key to adjust lon wire fading strength
