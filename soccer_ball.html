Secret language of Houdini

  • Nodes:
    • also known as Operators
  • SOPS:
    • Surface Operators (geometry
  • CHOPS:
    • Channel Operators (motions)
  • DOPS:
    • Dynamic Operators (simulation)
  • VOPS:
    • VEX Operators (shaders)
  • COPS:
    • Compositing Operators (compositing)
  • ROPS:
    • Render Operators (rendering)

Tips Along the Way

  • Shift-drag on handles:
    • centers transforms on center
  • Object level:
    • Manage transforms
  • Geometry level:
    • Manage shape
  • Esc for plain numbers, Space and number in any non-camera mode:
    • in Viewport to enable camera mode:
      • 1, 2, 3, 4:
        • to show perp/top/front/right
      • 5:
        • uv viewport
