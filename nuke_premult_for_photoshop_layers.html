Premulting Photoshop Layer Images

  • Photoshop performs premults in Photoshop within sRGB colorspace.
  • Exporting a layer from Photoshop, and importing to Nuke can result in an image that requires a premult operation (to simulate the transparency of the layer).
  • You must perform the premult in sRGB colorspace (rather than Linear) to get the correct edge results.
