Image Simplification

Massing
  • Massing areas with dark and light:
    • Munge
    • Blur
    • Wash

Darks can hold the local textures; burn out in brights

Image Simplification
  • Reduce detail in background:
    • hue variations
    • low frequency of detail
    • bring colors from foreground to simple areas:
      • unify objects in background
      • transition from back to front
Reducing Locals
  • Push bg to single color:
    • Dailes term: reduce local color
    • like a color wash
    • not desaturate, but push to bg wash color
Washes
  • Using washes of color (blues perhaps) for background or shadows
  • Region grads to transition between layering (almost like bounce light)
  • Lightening lines to help wash away details

Bilateral can really break up high frequency detailing; and looks more painterly

Edge Pooling

  • Created by edge detect on value separations (like sun to shadow masses).

Tech Tips

Clear Nuke Cache Often

Use Maps (Depth, RegionGrads, DeepMattes etc.) to mask areas to blur

Use wash or noise maps to vary as 'lost and found'

Use "Better Bloom" node to create a bounce/fill

Use Edge Pooling matte (blured) to create bounce/fill

Use edge pooling to bring wash detail back
