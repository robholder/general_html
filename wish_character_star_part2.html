Lighting Star - Part II

Passes

  • ch_star
  • ch_starlines
  • ch_starDust

Rules

  • Don't use cuttouts in DLight/renders.
  • hy_all_utility:
    • Star should be 'hidden'
    • Star appears as grainy signaling the error if it IS in the utility pass
  • Template takes all passes and sets up star char.
    • Update shotcam (Quip|Navigator panel)
    • Update Button node
    • click on update button:
      • It'll update locators
      • Screen space texture should be flat to view
  • Lighting:
    • driven by screen space actions/procedures
    • Theres a master Screen Space Blur node (Star Blur Master)
    • There's also a Star Body master.
      • In addition, there are Granular controls for eyes/eye-masks
  • Star's effects is largely driven by the alpha.
  • Star holdout mask is driven by depth and stuff.
    • These are piped in and multiplied.
    • Should all work out of the box.
  • FX passes control all the extras

Comp Holdouts

  • The holdouts are comp only so that blurs, glints, blooms can be controlled:
    • e.g., blur over foreground objects is less
    • all controlled in comp section
    • There is an optional section for adding glow back into cutout regions
  • All the cutout work should feed through to the paper texture section.
