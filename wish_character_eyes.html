Eyes

Eye treatment is all in the comp (Nuke) for WISH.

Comp Elements

  • WISH_EyeRig
    • SpecOnly
    • CausticOnly
    • EyeRigMattes

Eye Rig

  • Rig comes in with Breakout tool
  • Can be obtained from ShadowBox:
    • WISH:
      • filter for Eye:
        • WISH_Eye_Rig

Set up

  • Bring in WISH_EyeRig:
    • (As above)
  • Bring in Char geo:
    • Quip | Navigator:
      • char:
        • e.g., asha
  • Bring in stylized eye-specular map:
    • ShadowBox:
      • WISH:
        • filter for eye:
          • 'nuke_paintedLighting_double_eyeSpec'
    • NOTE: This is now in the comp with variations also.
  • From the WISH_EyeRig node:
    • 'usdgeo' pipe to imported char geo.
    • 'mattes' pipe to ch_mattes_inst
    • 'geo' pipe to ch_geometry_inst
    • 'specimg' to eyespec map (above)

Configure Eye Rig

WISH Eye Rig Tab

  • Click 'Configure Eye Rig'
    • (progress bar as it goes through framerange)
  • The position stuff at the bottom sets the card on the circumference/surface of the eye:
    • Don't change!
    • Stereo is ruined if wrong!
  • Reset Node:
    • Sets values to defaults
    • Clears animation
    • DOESN'T clear the configuration
    • Better than RMB - all knobs to default (because it's a group)

Spec Tab

  • Orientation:
    • 'Iris Aligned'
  • Adjust position on eye
  • Adjust scale (Shape)
  • "Keep Alive" - set to '0' for off
  • Adjust individual eyes (Right, Light):
    • These are 'deltas', so they are additive on the main controls

Caustic Tab

  • Caustics follow specs by default (toggle)
  • Exposure (brightness)
  • Size & Falloff
  • Offset (rotationally)
  • Erode:
    • adjusts edges on iris?
  • Right, Left deltas

Comp onto Character

  • ColorCorrect & Grades, then:
  • Plus the results (Spec & Caustic) onto the character:
    • 'Merge'
    • Plus

You can use the eye mattes to cut-out or adjust before merging

There may be a process for drop shadows from eyelids

  • Mattes can be utilized like any other mattes:
    • Red: Spec
    • Green: Caustic (contained in iris)
    • Blue: Caustic (goes beyond the iris boundary)

Watercolor & Paper

Further along, there's a section in the comp to deal with adding watercolor & paper texturing.

Propagation Issues

  • Propagated shot geo will point at the original shot's geo.
  • Keyed properties (static) might not work nicely.
  • If you have issues:
    • delete rig node
    • clear cache (Nuke)
    • import new instance from ShadowBox
